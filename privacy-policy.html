1. Who We Are

TikTok KPI Dashboard DZD (“we”, “us”, “our”) is an internal analytics dashboard used to retrieve and display TikTok video performance metrics for reporting and analysis purposes.

Contact: bkalem@ios.dz

2. Information We Collect

When you connect a TikTok account, we may collect and process:

  • Basic TikTok account information (depending on granted permissions)
  • Video data and performance metrics (e.g., video ID, title, create time, views, likes, shares, comments)
  • OAuth tokens required to access TikTok APIs (access and refresh tokens)
  • Technical logs for troubleshooting (timestamps, status codes, workflow run IDs)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Retrieve and update TikTok video KPI metrics
  • Store and display analytics data for internal reporting
  • Operate and monitor automation workflows (n8n)
  • Troubleshoot errors and improve reliability

4. Data Storage and Processing

Data may be stored and processed using:

  • TikTok APIs (data source)
  • n8n automation workflows
  • Microsoft 365 (Excel Online / OneDrive) for storage and reporting
  • Infrastructure providers hosting the website or workflows (if applicable)

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share data only with service providers necessary to operate the dashboard (e.g., TikTok, Microsoft 365, and hosting providers), and only as required to provide the service.

6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for analytics, reporting, troubleshooting, or legal/compliance obligations. You may request deletion of stored data by contacting us.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8. Your Choices and Rights

You may revoke TikTok authorization at any time through your TikTok account settings. Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data.

9. Children’s Privacy

This service is not intended for children and is not directed to individuals under the age required by applicable law.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
